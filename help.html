Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a game of skill. This is the ultimate battle between X and O.

How To Play

Find a friend. Choose who will be X and who will be O.

In the first game X goes first. The first mover alternates with each round.

If you win, there is crazy celebrations. But you can't rest on your laurels. Hit the reset button at the bottom the screen and get into the next round.

You can keep an eye on who is the ultimate champion with the score card at the top of the screen.

Frustrated that you are not winning? Not a problem. Hit refresh on your browser and you start all over again with all scores back at zero.

How Important Is This

Similar to the movie 'The Last Starfighter', if you are a mega champion at this (and we have algorithms to check), then one day we might select you to take on Deep Blue in a game to the death.


Enjoy!
